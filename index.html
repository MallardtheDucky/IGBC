Key Functions of IGBC-Net

Real-Time Interplanetary Settlements

Utilizes asynchronous consensus protocols for transactions across colonies like Mars and Europa, reconciling with signal lag.

Multi-Ledger Settlement Layer

Features separate chains for planetary, orbital, and interplanetary settlements, all settled in UC pegged to commodities.

Smart Escrow & Contract System

Programmable contracts with escrow payments and auto-verification using sensor data.

Quantum-Safe Encryption & Redundancy

Protected by quantum-safe encryption with redundant validation nodes across colonies.

AI-Assisted Compliance and Risk Tracking

Automates compliance and detects fraud with AI monitoring anomalous patterns.

Decentralized but Governed

Decentralized validation with IGBC retaining governance rights over validators and fees.
