About IGBC

Founded in the wake of the 2197 Economic Crisis, the InterGalactic Banking Consortium (IGBC) stabilizes and centralizes interplanetary finance. Headquartered on Earth, we facilitate currency exchanges, trade settlements, and fund critical space infrastructure projects, driving humanity's expansion across the Solaris system.
